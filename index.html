About me

Hi everyone!
I'm Davide Russo from Naples, Italy.
I got a Master's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Naples Federico II, my thesis was about Emotion Recognition based on EEG signals classification.
As you can see I've always been interested in Machine Learning and working in the data fields; after my graduation I started to work for Techedge Group in Milan (Italy) where I joined the data science team as Consultant.
If you want to know more about me, have a look at my updated resume
